Neurology Department

Advanced treatment for neurological disorders affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nerves.

Our Specialized Care Units

Comprehensive Stroke Center

Time is brain. Our rapid-response stroke team is equipped with the latest neuro-imaging and life-saving intervention techniques available 24/7.

Epilepsy & Seizure Care

Personalized treatment plans including video-EEG monitoring and advanced medication management to help patients gain control over seizures.

A dedicated team of therapists helping patients recover motor and cognitive functions following surgery, injury, or neurological illness.
