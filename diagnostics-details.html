Our Diagnostic Services

MRI & CT Imaging

Our facility features high-tesla MRI and multi-slice CT scanners that provide crystal-clear internal images, allowing for earlier detection of health issues.

Digital Radiology

We use fully digital X-ray technology, which results in lower radiation exposure and instant image availability for your medical team.

Pathology Services

Our on-site laboratory handles blood work, biopsies, and molecular testing with high accuracy and industry-leading turnaround times.
